Learn from the Best

Open Courseware

Full courses from MIT, Harvard, Stanford, and more — free to audit, no enrolment needed.

Top Pick

MIT OpenCourseWare

All Levels

Complete lecture notes, problem sets, exams, and videos from MIT's entire course catalog — no sign-up required.

ocw.mit.edu

Coursera (Audit Free)

All Levels

Audit thousands of university-level courses from Yale, Duke, Stanford, and more at no cost — click "Audit" at enrolment.

coursera.org

edX

Bachelor Master · PhD

Free courses from Harvard, MIT, and 160+ institutions — covering engineering, business, humanities, and data science.

edx.org

NPTEL

Bachelor Master · PhD

High-quality engineering, science, and management lectures from India's top IIT and IISc institutions — free and certifiable.

nptel.ac.in
Read for Free

Textbooks & Reading

Free peer-reviewed textbooks, open-access journals, and millions of academic texts.

Top Pick

OpenStax

Bachelor

Peer-reviewed, freely available textbooks for introductory courses in physics, biology, chemistry, economics, and more.

openstax.org

DOAJ — Open Access Journals

Master · PhD

Directory of thousands of peer-reviewed open-access academic journals across every discipline — no paywall.

doaj.org

Internet Archive — Books

All Levels

Borrow and read millions of digitised books and academic texts online for free with a free account.

archive.org

Project Gutenberg

All Levels

Over 70,000 free eBooks — essential reading for humanities, literature, history, and philosophy students.

gutenberg.org
Find & Cite

Research & Citation

Search scholarly literature, organise sources, and visualise connections between papers.

Top Pick

Google Scholar

All Levels

Search millions of scholarly articles, theses, books, and conference papers across all disciplines — free.

scholar.google.com

Zotero

All Levels

Free reference manager to collect, organise, cite, and share sources — with browser plugins for one-click saving.

zotero.org

Semantic Scholar

All Levels

AI-powered academic search that surfaces the most influential and relevant research, with citation context.

semanticscholar.org

Connected Papers

Master · PhD

Visualise the web of academic papers around any work — perfect for mapping a field before writing a literature review.

connectedpapers.com
Write & Organise

Writing & Productivity

Write better essays, manage your knowledge, and stay organised through your degree.

Top Pick

Notion

All Levels

All-in-one workspace for notes, project planning, databases, and collaboration — free for students with an edu email.

notion.so

Overleaf

Bachelor Master · PhD

Free online LaTeX editor for writing academic papers, theses, and scientific reports — real-time collaboration built in.

overleaf.com

Obsidian

All Levels

Powerful linked note-taking app for building a personal knowledge base — connects ideas across all your notes.

obsidian.md

Grammarly

All Levels

AI writing assistant that checks grammar, clarity, tone, and style — free browser extension works everywhere you write.

grammarly.com
Study Smarter

AI Study Tools

AI assistants that help you research, write, understand, and prepare for exams — use them responsibly.

Top Pick

NotebookLM

All Levels

Upload lecture notes, papers, and PDFs — AI generates summaries, answers your questions, and creates study guides from your own material.

notebooklm.google.com

Consensus

Master · PhD

Ask a research question and get AI-summarised findings from peer-reviewed papers — ideal for literature reviews.

consensus.app

Perplexity AI

All Levels

AI-powered search with cited, sourced answers — great for quick academic queries without leaving a browser tab.

perplexity.ai

Claude AI

All Levels

Excellent for deep analysis, long-document understanding, essay feedback, and working through complex academic concepts.

claude.ai

Google Colab

colab.research.google.com

Kaggle

All Levels

Free datasets, notebooks, competitions, and courses to learn data science and machine learning completely hands-on.

kaggle.com

freeCodeCamp
